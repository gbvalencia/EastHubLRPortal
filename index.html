Guidelines

Logging in to the LR Portal using LRN and default password

To log in to the LR Portal, follow these steps:

  1. On the LR Portal login page, locate the login form.
  2. Choose As student for Students and As Teacher for Teachers
  3. For student, enter your Learner Reference Number (LRN) in the designated field. The LRN is a unique identification number assigned to learners.
  4. Enter the default password in the password field. The default password may come from your adviser.
  5. Click on the "Sign In" or "Login" button to proceed.
  6. For teacher, enter your seven-digit employee number in the designated field.
  7. Enter the default password in the password field. The default password may come from school ICT Coordinators.
  8. Click on the "Sign In" or "Login" button to proceed.
  9. If this is your first time logging in with the default school user account, you may be prompted to change your default password for security reasons. Follow the instructions provided on the screen to update your password. Typically, you will need to enter your new password twice to confirm it.
  10. Once you have successfully logged in, you will be directed to the LR Portal dashboard or main page, where you can access various learning resources or perform other tasks specific to the LR Portal.

Remember to change your default password to a secure and unique password after logging in for the first time to ensure the security of your account.
